<p>Configure your preferred DNS modes to toggle in app and toggle your Private DNS mode from your quick settings panel.</p><p>In order to change the Private DNS setting, this app requires the ’WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS’ permission.</p><p>This permission can only be granted in an ADB shell with the command:<br><code>pm grant com.flashsphere.privatednsqs android.permission.WRITE_SECURE_SETTINGS</code><br>Check out <a href='https://private-dns-qs.web.app/help' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>private-dns-qs.web.app/help</a> on the steps to use adb to grant the permission.</p>
